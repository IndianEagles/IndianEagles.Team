Leadership Productivity Bonus: 15% Pool

  • This bonus as the name suggests is paid as a reward to create leaders in your team
  • Modicare set's aside 15% of company's monthly BV as Leadership Productivity Bonus (LPB)
  • It is paid to all Qualified Senior Director and above consultants who have at least one leg of 90,000 GBV and a Senior Director or above in the same

  • If your Senior Director downline does not have a GBV of 90,000 then GBV of qualified/unqualified Director consultants is rolled up till 90,000 GBV is achieved
    Rules:
  • A Qualifying up-line Senior Director and above can earn LPB only on all Qualified Senior Director and above downlines' PGBV upto 8 generations
  • In order to earn on all the qualifying generations in your depth (as mentioned in the LPB table) you must maintain a title higher than all qualifying downlines, however in case your Downline in the LPB qualification leg is at equal or higher paid as title than you, then you will be paid only on one more generation under him/her
  • Qualified Senior Director and above is eligible to receive a Guarantee of 4,500 points from all legs with a GBV of atleast 90,000 and a Qualified Senior Director or above in those legs
  • All Qualified Senior Director and above consultants who are eligible to earn LPB must give a "Guarantee" of 4,500 points to the next Qualifying Senior Director and above up-line
  • Non Qualified Director volumes will be considered for LPB earnings only in the legs which qualify for LPB
  • LPB is computed through a "Point System" based on LPB Points earned by all qualifying Senior Director and above achievers
  • LPB point index would be allotted on the bases of monthly computation and is likely to vary from month to month and would be determined and announced after the end of the month
  • The formula for determining the LPB Points is mentioned below
  • Modicare's total monthly BV = A 
  • Leadership Productivity Bonus Pool (15% of A) = B 
  • Sum of total all LPB points collected by all LPB qualifiers in a month = C 
  • LPB Point Index (Rupees) = B/C
