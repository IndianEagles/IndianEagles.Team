  • To qualify for performance bonus, a consultant should do a minimum personal purchase of 300 PBV till the level of the Supervisor and at least 600 PBV from Director level onwards.
  • If in any case the consultant fails to make the required minimum personal purchase, the Accumulative Performance Bonus earned in that month is carried forward and is released in the month the consultant makes the required minimum personal purchase.
