Director Bonus: 14% Pool

  • Modicare sets aside 14% of Company's monthly BV as Director Bonus (DB) Pool
  • All qualified Directors & above consultants can earn this bonus upto 9 generations
  • This is computed by "Point System" based on Director Bonus points earned by all qualifying "Directors and above" achievers
  • DB Point index is allotted on the basis of monthly consumption and is likely to vary from month to month; the index is determined and announced after the end of the month
  • The formula for determining the DB Points is mentioned below
  • Modicare's total monthly BV = A 
  • Director Bonus Pool (14% of A) = B 
  • Sum of total DB points collected by all DB qualifiers in a month = C 
  • DB Point Index (Rupees) = B/C
